Cleaner than a big hero embed

Landing stays clean. Tutorials get their own premium space.

Use a simple CTA on the landing page and send users to a full tutorial subpage with videos, categories, progress and “try in app” actions.

Why this is the cleaner structure

Your landing page should sell the product. Your tutorial subpage should explain the product. Splitting those two jobs almost always looks more premium and converts better for complex software.

Cleaner hero

No awkward big embedded player fighting with the headline. Just a strong CTA like “Watch demo” or “Open tutorial hub”.

Better onboarding

Users can go from “What is this?” to “How do I use it?” in a structured path: Getting Started → Editor → AI Studio → Publish.

Scales with your product

As you add more features, you can keep expanding the Learn page without bloating the landing page every time.
