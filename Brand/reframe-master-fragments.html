Category framing

Reframe is not just a builder. It is a workflow layer for agencies and teams that need to take over existing websites, turn them into structured projects, and run edits, localization, AI, and delivery from one place.

Why now

Website work is still fragmented across builders, plugins, docs, PM tools, and AI chats. The stack is overdue for a system that connects the real website, the workflow, and the intelligence layer.

Moat angle

If Reframe becomes the system agencies use to ingest, restructure, edit, and deliver websites, it can own more of the operational surface than tools that focus only on design or only on AI chat.
